# **App Name**: AmplifyAI

## Core Features:

- Brand DNA Extraction: Extract brand color palette, typography, tone of voice, and style from website URLs, social profiles, customer reviews, and competitor URLs. It leverages Google Gemini as a tool to conduct a multimodal analysis.
- Multi-Format Content Generation: Generate platform-optimized content across text, visual, video and audio formats, including social posts, blog articles, display ads, short-form reels, and podcast scripts. This feature leverages Google Gemini to assist with content creation.
- Campaign Orchestration Dashboard: Provide a cross-platform content calendar with drag-and-drop scheduling, platform-specific optimization (Instagram vs LinkedIn vs TikTok), and A/B test generation. This may include suggested prediction using Google Gemini, in the role of a tool.
- Direct Publishing & Integration: Enable direct API integration with Meta (Instagram/Facebook), LinkedIn, X, TikTok, Google Ads, and YouTube.  Offer automated posting with timezone optimization.
- Analytics & Performance Tracking: Implement a real-time dashboard for tracking reach, engagement, clicks, and conversions. Calculate campaign ROI, optimize budgets, and use AI feedback to refine future content.
- Competitor Monitoring: Web scraping to for monitoring of competitor campaigns, trending topic alerts and seasonal campaign suggestions

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Amplify Orange (#F97316), drawing on the name 'Amplify' and brand association with energy and collaboration, a vibrant and eye-catching hue.
- Background color: Light orange (#FFF7ED), a very light tint of orange that maintains a warm, energetic connection to the brand but does not distract.
- Accent color: Coral (#FF6B5B), used sparingly for highlighting interactive elements.
- Headlines: 'Playfair Display' serif for an elegant and sophisticated look.
- Body: 'Inter' sans-serif for clean and readable text. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Modern, flat design icons with a focus on clarity.  Use brand colors sparingly to highlight key actions. Ensure icons are scalable and recognizable.
- Clean, minimal, mobile-first layout with a clear visual hierarchy.  Generous white space, clear section dividers, and consistent padding.
- Subtle animations, particularly scroll animations, and loading states. Use parallax scrolling effects for background elements.
